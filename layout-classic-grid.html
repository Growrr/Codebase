Incorporate the Codebase semantic grid mixins into your own layout, using your own choice of class names. 

<section class="grid grid--gutter">
  <div class="column medium--span-8 large--push-4">
    Article

    <section class="grid grid--gutter grid--nested">
      <div class="column small--span-4">
        Nested
      </div>
      <div class="column small--span-4">
        Nested
      </div>
      <div class="column small--span-4">
        Nested
      </div>
    </section>

  </div>
  <div class="column medium--span-4 large--pull-8">
    Aside
  </div>
</section>

<section class="grid grid--gutter">
  <div class="column small--span-7 column--centered">
    Centered
  </div>
</section>
Class Explanation
.grid The containing class. Sets the max width (from variable $grid-max-width), does centering by auto margin right and left, and includes a clearfix.
.grid--gutter Gives right and left padding to all immediate child block elements (i.e. columns).
.grid--nested Gives negative right and left margin to the grid container equal to the gutter width, so that a nested grid is flush right and left inside their containing column.
.column Float left.
.column--centered No float. Instead, centering is accomplished by auto margin right and left.
.tiny--*
.small--*
.medium--*
.large--* 		Controlling the column behaviour at different breakpoints.
Except for .tiny--* all columns expand to 100% of their container width below their breakpoint.
*--span-* Column spanning (at different breakpoints), as calculated out of max $grid-max-columns (default is 12).
*--push-* Column pushing to the right (at different breakpoints), as calculated out of max $grid-max-columns (default is 12).
*--pull-* Column pullling to the left (at different breakpoints), as calculated out of max $grid-max-columns (default is 12).
*--offset-* Column offsetting from the left (at different breakpoints), as calculated out of max $grid-max-columns (default is 12).
